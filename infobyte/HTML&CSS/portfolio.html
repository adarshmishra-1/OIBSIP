About Me

I am a freelance professional specializing in Educational Technology (EdTech). With a passion for combining education and technology, I provide innovative solutions that enhance the learning experience of my clients.

Throughout my career, I have worked on various EdTech projects, collaborating with educators, instructional designers, and developers to create interactive and engaging learning platforms. I have experience designing and developing educational software, learning management systems, and e-learning applications.

My skill set includes:

  • Educational software development
  • Learning management system (LMS) customization and integration
  • Content creation and curriculum design
  • User interface (UI) and user experience (UX) design
  • E-learning platform development
  • Mobile app development for educational purposes

I am passionate about leveraging technology to transform education and empower learners. I stay up-to-date with the latest trends and advancements in EdTech to ensure that my solutions align with the evolving needs of educators and learners.
